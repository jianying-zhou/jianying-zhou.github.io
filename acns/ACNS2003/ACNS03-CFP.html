ACNS 2003

International Conference on

Kunming, China
October 16-19, 2003


CALL FOR PAPERS

[extended submission deadline:  May 18, 2003]


The International Communications and Information Security Association (ICISA) has organized successful events of ICICS'97 in Beijing, ICICS'99 in Sydney, ICICS'01 in Xian and ICICS'02 in Singapore. To follow the progress of academic and industrial security, ICISA will continue the ICICS series and initiate a new conference series ACNS. The first MiAn International Conference on “Applied Cryptography and Network Security” (ACNS’03) will be held in Kunming, China on October 16-19, 2003, organized by MiAn (ONETS) Pte Ltd and in cooperation with the local government.

Conference Topics

Original paper on all aspects of applied cryptography and network security are solicited for submission to the conference. Areas of interests include but not restricted to

Biometric Security Applications
Cryptanalysis and Anti-Cryptanalysis
Cryptographic Applications
Data Recovery and Coding
Efficient Implementation
Firewall and Intrusion Detection
GPRS and CDMA Security
Identification and Entity Authentication
Key Management Techniques
Network Protocol and Analysis
 Non-repudiation and Cryptographic Evidence
PKI/PMI and Bridge CA
Secure e-commerce and e-government
Security Management and Strategy
Side Channel Cryptanalysis
Smart Card Security
Verification and Testing of Secure Systems
Viruses
VPN and SVN
WLAN and Bluetooth Security

Conference Proceedings

The conference proceedings of the academic part will be published in Springer's Lecture Notes in Computer Science series and available at the conference. Industrial solution proposals will be published by ICISA in a separate proceedings and should also be available at the conference.

Instruction for Authors

Both papers and industrial solution proposals are welcome. All submissions must be anonymous, with no author names, affiliations, acknowledgements, or obvious references. It should begin with a title, a short abstract, and a list of keywords. A limit of 12 single-spaced pages of 11pt font is placed on all submissions. All submissions are via email as a MIME attachment in PDF or PS format.
  • Send papers to acns2003@i2r.a-star.edu.sg with a subject line “ACNS’03 paper submission”.
  • Send industrial solution proposals to onets@onets.com.cn with a subject line “ACNS’03 proposal submission”.
In the same email, also include (in plain ASCII text format) the title and abstract, the authors' names, email and postal addresses, phone and fax numbers, and identification of the contact author.

An acknowledgment email will be sent to the corresponding author upon receiving each submission. The authors are advised to send an inquiry to the email address used in submission if they do not receive the acknowledgement by May 20, 2003.

The authors of all submissions are urged to comply with the Springer’s authors instructions which are accessible from http://www.springer.de/comp/lncs/authors.html.
