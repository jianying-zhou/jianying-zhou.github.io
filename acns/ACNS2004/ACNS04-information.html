MAKING HOTEL RESERVATIONS
 
You should book a room directly with the conference hotel www.huangshaninterhotel.com. The conference rate is 260 RMB (or approximately 35 USD) per night for a standard room. In order to obtain the conference rate, please register by fax at 86-559-2512857, attention Miss Xu Meimei, identifying yourself as a participant of ACNS04.

OBTAINING VISA
 
Visa is required for people from most of foreign countries. Please contact the organizing committee for an invitation letter.


GETTING THERE
 
The airport closest to the conference hotel is Yellow Mountain Airport, also referred to as HuangShan Airport or TunXi Airport. The airport code is TXN. It has daily connections from Beijing, Shanghai and Guangzhou. A taxi from the airport to the conference hotel is approximately 15 RMB (or approximately 2 USD).
