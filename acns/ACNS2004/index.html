ACNS 2004

Second International Conference on
Applied Cryptography and Network Security

Yellow Mountain, China
June 8-11, 2004



The conference has two parallel tracks: academic and technical. The papers being accepted for presentation at the academic track are included in the Springer's LNCS 3089, while the papers being accepted for presentation at the technical track are published as a special issue in the Journal of China Information Security.
