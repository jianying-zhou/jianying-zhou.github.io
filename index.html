Biography

Jianying Zhou is a professor and center director for iTrust at Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD). Before joining SUTD, he was a principal scientist and head of Infocomm Security Department at Institute for Infocomm Research, A*STAR. He also worked at the headquarters of Oracle as a security consultant.

Prof. Zhou received his PhD in Information Security from Royal Holloway, University of London. His research interests are in applied cryptography and network security, cyber-physical system security, and mobile and wireless security. He has published 300+ referred papers at top venues including IEEE S&P, ACM CCS, USENIX Security, NDSS, IEEE TIFS, and IEEE TDSC. He received the ESORICS'15 Best Paper Award and the ACSAC'23 Distinguished Paper Award. He has 2 technologies standardized in ISO/IEC 29192-4 and ISO/IEC 20009-4, and holds 10+ patents.

Prof. Zhou is a co-founder and steering committee co-chair of ACNS, and steering committee chair of ACM AsiaCCS. He serves as Associate Editor-in-Chief of IEEE Security & Privacy. He is an IEEE Fellow (Class of 2026) and an ACM Distinguished Member. He received the ESORICS Outstanding Contribution Award in 2020.
